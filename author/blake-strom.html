Blake Strom

Blake Strom is the Capability Area Lead for Adversary Emulation at MITRE and has worked in the areas of network defense, cyber threat intelligence, security research, and adversary emulation. Blake is a co-creator of ATT&CK has been leading the project since its inception. Blake also leads the CALDERA research project to automate adversary emulation. He advocates for security through verification at all points at which an adversary could be detected or stopped because defenders should not wait for a real intrusion to see if their methods work. Blake is a graduate of the Computer Science program at the University of California, Berkeley. Blake Strom can be contacted here>.
